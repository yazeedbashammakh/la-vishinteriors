About US

LA-VISH INTERIORS is respected turnkey interior design firm that caters its service in Thane, Mumbai and Navi Mumbai. We specialize in assimilating innovative products and concepts that are simply elegant and stylish and often surpass the industry benchmarks. We come up with extremely innovative ideas for interior spaces of our clients and use various latest technologies to gear up the design by focusing on practical application and implications of our concepts.

We work with dedication and take into consideration minute details of our project thus achieving the ultimate contemporary style with special focus on decoration, space usage, furniture, interior lighting etc. of contemporary design. We are customer centric as we undertake single project and create designs that reflects the style of customer. Our projects speak for themselves and our clients speak for us. Interior Design solution by us won't be simple rather it would be something that is rich by look and lighter on budget so contact us for your dream space design.
